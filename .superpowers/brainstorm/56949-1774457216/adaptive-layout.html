The 8-Step Adaptive Experience

  • Steps 1-3 (Preparation): Sidebar is **Expanded** with text labels. Easy to browse sensors and library.
  • Steps 4-5 (Setup): Sidebar remains expanded. The main area shows the "Pre-Flight" dashboard.
  • Step 6 (Active Workout): Sidebar automatically **Collapses** to "Icon-Only" (as shown above) or **Hides** completely.
  • Step 7-8 (End/Save): Sidebar **Expands** again to show "History" and "Export" options clearly.
